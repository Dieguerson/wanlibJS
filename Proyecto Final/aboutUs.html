About Us

Woric Leatherbound:

The proverbial Wandering Librarian. Obsesive as any good dwarf and a bookworm, Woric's destiny was sealed the day he layed eyes on his first book. A few dacades later he would meet a tired Mr. Whiskers looking to retire. The rest, as they say, is history.

The Infinite Archive:

The Archive began it's existance as a small library owned by a small family on a small town. The youngest of the family would become Mr. Whiskers and the library... well, it would become the Archive. Technically not part of the staff. But try explaining that to a sentient library.

Mr. Whiskers:

Ex-Sorcerer Extraordinaire turned full time lazy cat. Only Woric knows Mr. Whiskers' real name and is sworn to secrecy. After many, many years of building the largest most amazing sentient library, just when he was ready to retire he met Woric, a young book loving Dwarf, a perfect succesor.

Traner:

As every gnome, Traner has many, many, many names and nicknames. So we use Traner for short. Our most esteemed accountant, a brilliant mind for numbers and an undying lust for wandering. If the bussines makes any money it's because of his entrepeneurship and wits.

Boro and Orbo:

Dwarven trained ponies won't stop walking until their masters say so or the fall form exhaustyion. Boro and Orbo won't stop even at that, tirelessly pulling the cart that houses the Archive.
